Nigel's Library

Nigel William Minton

1948 – 2024

Welcome to Nigel's Library — a durable digital record of the books, music and films collected by Nigel William Minton over his lifetime, registered and recorded by Nigel himself in his LibraryThing account, and now gradually finding new homes.

The library is being catalogued and cared for. A page for each item will appear here as the collection finds its new homes.

You can write to the family at jon.will.minton@gmail.com
